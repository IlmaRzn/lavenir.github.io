ABOUT US

LCE is one of the leading multidisciplinary consultancy offices in Doha, known for its high standards, reliability, and client-focused approach. Since its establishment in 2010, LCE has been providing professional project management and engineering consultancy services for a wide range of projects. Our firm is led by the respected Eng. Ahmad Hindawi, a Grade A Civil Engineer with over 35 years of experience in the field. Under his leadership, LCE has built a reputation for excellence and innovation in the construction industry.

We take pride in our team of highly qualified Grade A Engineers across key disciplines, including Architecture, Civil, and Electrical engineering. This enables us to offer fully integrated solutions tailored to the specific needs of each client and project. Whether it's a residential development, a commercial complex, or an industrial facility, LCE ensures every aspect is managed with precision and professionalism.

Our core services include high-quality 3D exterior and interior design, providing clients with realistic visualizations to better understand and refine their concepts before implementation. We also specialize in building permit and modification works, assisting clients with the preparation of technical drawings and the submission process to secure necessary approvals efficiently. Additionally, our on-site supervision services ensure that projects are executed in line with design specifications, safety standards, and regulatory requirements, maintaining the highest level of quality throughout the construction process.
