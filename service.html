OUR SERVICES

At LCE, we take pride in delivering a comprehensive range of professional engineering and consultancy services tailored to meet the diverse needs of our clients. Our expertise spans across various disciplines, ensuring seamless integration and top-tier quality in every project we undertake.

Architectural, Structural, MEP Drawings & Civil Defense Compliance

We specialize in the preparation of detailed Architectural, Structural, and MEP (Mechanical, Electrical, and Plumbing) drawings that align with local regulations. We ensure full Civil Defense compliance with fire safety systems and technical documentation to meet safety standards.

Supervision Services

Our team offers on-site project supervision and technical oversight to ensure smooth execution, adherence to design specifications, and compliance with regulatory codes.

3D Exterior & Interior Design

Bring your vision to life with high-quality 3D rendering. We offer photorealistic visualizations for exterior and interior designs that reflect your unique style and goals.

Building Permit & Modification Works

We assist with building permit applications and modification works. Our experts streamline approvals and ensure compliance with all technical and legal standards.
