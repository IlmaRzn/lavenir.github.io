Civil Defence Drawings Approval Process in Qatar

  1. CLIENT MEETING & PROJECT INITIATION
  2. UNDERSTANDING PROJECT REQUIREMENTS
    • Type of building (villa, commercial, industrial, etc.)
    • Civil Defence compliance needs
    • Plot area, building height, occupancy type
  3. COLLECTION OF NECESSARY DOCUMENTS
    • Land ownership certificate
    • Qatar ID copy
    • CR copy (for commercial)
    • Municipality zoning approval
  4. ARCHITECTURAL DESIGN STAGE
    • Prepare base architectural plans (floor plans, elevations, sections)
  5. CIVIL DEFENCE COMPLIANCE DESIGN
    • Fire safety features: exits, fire alarm systems, sprinklers, fire hose reels, emergency lighting, etc.
    • Coordination with MEP team for fire protection layouts
  6. INTERNAL REVIEW BY CONSULTANT
    • Ensure compliance with QCDD (Qatar Civil Defence Department) codes
    • Check escape routes, fire compartments, access for firefighting
  7. CLIENT REVIEW & APPROVAL OF DRAWINGS
    • Present preliminary design for client feedback
  8. MODIFICATION/REVISIONS IF REQUIRED
    • Make changes based on client or internal reviews
  9. FINALIZE DRAWINGS FOR SUBMISSION
    • Architectural, firefighting, fire alarm, and emergency lighting plans
    • Supporting documents: compliance statements, calculations, etc.
  10. ONLINE SUBMISSION TO QCDD
    • Through the MEHEKMA system
    • Upload all approved drawings, documents, and design justifications
  11. INITIAL REVIEW BY QCDD ENGINEERS
    • They check code compliance, layout accuracy, fire safety access, etc.
  12. COMMENTS/REJECTION (IF ANY)
    • QCDD provides feedback or required corrections
  13. MODIFICATION STAGE (IF NEEDED)
    • Consultant revises drawings based on QCDD comments
    • Update design, resubmit through MEHEKMA
  14. RESUBMISSION TO QCDD
    • With updated documents and highlighted changes
  15. FINAL APPROVAL FROM CIVIL DEFENCE
    • QCDD issues stamped approved drawings
  16. MUNICIPALITY BUILDING PERMIT APPLICATION
    • Submit approved QCDD drawings to Baladiya (Municipality)
  17. OBTAIN BUILDING PERMIT
    • Building construction can officially begin
