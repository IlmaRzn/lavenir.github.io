OUR SERVICES

At LCE, we take pride in delivering a comprehensive range of professional engineering and consultancy services tailored to meet the diverse needs of our clients. Our expertise spans across various disciplines, ensuring seamless integration and top-tier quality in every project we undertake.

Architectural, Structural, MEP Drawings & Civil Defense Compliance

We specialize in the preparation of detailed Architectural, Structural, and MEP (Mechanical, Electrical, and Plumbing) drawings that align with local regulations, including Electrical, Water Supply, Drainage, and Ooredoo infrastructure layouts required for Municipality approvals. Additionally, we ensure full Civil Defense compliance by integrating fire safety systems and providing all necessary technical documentation to meet regulatory and safety standards.

Supervision Services

Our qualified team offers on-site project supervision and technical oversight to ensure smooth execution, adherence to design specifications, and compliance with regulatory codes. We actively monitor all phases of construction to uphold safety standards, optimize workflows, and deliver superior results.

3D Exterior & Interior Design

Bring your vision to life with our high-quality 3D rendering and design services. We create photorealistic visualizations for both exterior and interior spaces, allowing clients to preview and refine their projects before construction begins. Our designs are tailored to reflect your style, functionality needs, and project goals.

Permit & Modification Works

We provide end-to-end assistance with building permit applications and modification works. Our team works closely with relevant authorities to streamline approval processes, ensuring all documentation meets the necessary legal and technical criteria. Whether you're initiating a new project or altering an existing structure, we help you navigate regulatory requirements with confidence.
