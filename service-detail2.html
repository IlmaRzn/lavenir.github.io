Structural Drawings Submission & Approval Process in Qatar

1. Initial Meeting with Client
Understand project type, scope, design expectations, and required structural systems (concrete, steel, etc.).
↓
2. Review of Architectural Drawings
Analyze approved architectural layouts to plan structural grid and load paths.
↓
3. Soil Investigation & Site Analysis
Conduct geotechnical surveys and soil testing to determine foundation design requirements.
↓
4. Preliminary Structural Design Proposal
Develop initial structural scheme (foundation, column layout, slab type, etc.).
↓
5. Client & Architect Coordination
Ensure structural design aligns with architectural vision and functional needs.
↓
6. Structural Calculations & Detailed Drawings Development
Prepare full structural analysis reports, design calculations, and detailed construction drawings:
- Foundations
- Columns & Beams
- Slabs & Roofs
- Shear walls, etc.
↓
7. Internal Coordination with MEP & Architectural Teams
Ensure there are no clashes and proper service integration.
↓
8. Pre-Submission Check for Compliance
Ensure all drawings follow Qatar Construction Specifications (QCS), municipality standards, and safety codes.
↓
9. Submission to Municipality / OBPS (Online Building Permit System)
Submit the structural drawings with supporting documents (calculation reports, soil test, architectural drawings, etc.).
↓
10. Municipality Structural Review
Baladiya reviews structural safety, load capacities, and code compliance.
↓
11. Comments / Required Modifications
If issues are found, feedback is given requiring changes or clarification.
↓
12. Revisions to Structural Drawings
Update structural plans and reports according to feedback. Coordinate changes with the rest of the design team.
↓
13. Resubmission of Corrected Drawings
Send the revised drawings for re-evaluation.
↓
14. Final Approval of Structural Drawings
Approval is granted when all requirements are satisfied.
↓
15. Final Approval of Building Permit
Once architectural, structural, and MEP drawings are approved, the building permit is issued.
