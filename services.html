WHAT LAVANIER CONSULTANT ENGINEERING CAN DO FOR YOU

At Lavanier Consultant Engineering (LCE), we provide comprehensive engineering solutions tailored to meet your project needs with precision and efficiency. Our expertise spans across multiple disciplines, ensuring seamless approvals, innovative designs, and top-tier supervision for every stage of your project.

Engineering Drawings, Approvals & Civil Defense Compliance

We prepare detailed Architectural, Structural, and MEP (Electrical, Water, Drainage & Ooredoo) drawings to meet Municipality requirements. Our team also provides compliant Civil Defense drawings, ensuring smooth and timely approvals for your projects.

Exterior & Interior 3D Designs

Our expert designers create high-quality 3D visualizations for both interior and exterior spaces, helping clients envision their projects before construction begins. We focus on aesthetics, functionality, and innovation, bringing concepts to life with stunning realism.

Building Permit Modifications

We handle all building permit modification works, ensuring that your project remains compliant with updated regulations and meets all necessary requirements for approvals and construction changes.

Supervision Services

Our dedicated supervision team ensures that every project is executed with precision and quality. From planning to completion, we oversee construction activities, ensuring adherence to design specifications, safety standards, and project timelines.
